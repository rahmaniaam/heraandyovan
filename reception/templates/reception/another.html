The Story of Us

We never remember our first meeting. Since it was years ago, and we didn’t even care. So it wasn’t love at first sight. Nor was it any other fairytale stories. But God’s fate has brought us together and it was more than magical!

Our grand families come from the same root, the infamous Kotogadang, West Sumatra. So our families knew one another. We even have mutual relatives. The funny thing is, we only started to recognize each other back in 2013.

After almost 5 years of relationship, partially long distance, we decided to bring it to the next level. And now here we are, just a few days before our wedding day.
